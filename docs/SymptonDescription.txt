A: No repiratory or Asthma Symptoms
    - No coughing, wheezing, or shortness of breath
    - No chest tightness
    - No need for rescue inhaler
    - Able to exercise and sleep normally
B: Few Respiratory or Asthma Symptoms
    - Coughing or wheezing once in a while
    - Mild shortness of breath with exercise
    - Symptoms occur few times per week
    - May occasionally need rescue inhaler
C: Daily repiratory or Asthma Symptoms
    - Coughing, wheezing, or chest tightness Daily
    - Shortness of breath with normal activities
    - Frequent use of rescue inhaler
    - Symptoms that disturb sleep
    - Limited exercise
